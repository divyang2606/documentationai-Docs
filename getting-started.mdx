## Overview

SproutOS takes you from a project idea to an export-ready website design in one structured flow. You start with a text prompt or a Guided Brief on the dashboard. SproutOS generates your sitemap automatically. You then customize the design and export to Elementor, Gutenberg, Figma, or React. Brief, sitemap, design, and export all happen in one place. No separate tools needed.

## Before You Start

- A SproutOS account. Sign up at `https://sproutos.ai` if you don't have one

- A basic description of the website you want to build

## Step 1: Log In to the Dashboard

Go to `https://dashboard.sproutos.ai` and log in with your email or Google account.

<Image src="/images/getting-started/dashboard-overview.png" width="800" height="450" alt="SproutOS dashboard showing the prompt box in the center and recent projects on the left sidebar" />

You land on the main dashboard. Your recent projects appear on the left. The prompt box sits in the center of the screen.

## Step 2: Start Your Project

SproutOS gives you two ways to start. Both generate a sitemap. Choose based on how much detail you need upfront.

**Option 1: Quick Prompt**

Type a short description of the website in the prompt box. One or two sentences is enough.

<Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-631be872-18d4-4adb-9443-cc16a0d6b8aa/doc-0ef24a17-abe4-4492-a600-867502a0482d/1775195473353-pnsgnvvc3hd-SproutOS-Dash.webp" width="800" height="400" alt="SproutOS prompt box with example text 'Make a website for a restaurant' and the Generate button" />

Click **Generate**. SproutOS uses your prompt to build a sitemap immediately.

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Not sure what to write? Click **Spin up an idea** below the prompt box to get a starting suggestion.
</Callout>

**Option 2: Guided Brief**

Click **Start with a Guided Brief** below the prompt box. This opens a step-by-step questionnaire. You answer questions about the project goals, target audience, brand direction, and references.

<Image src="/images/getting-started/guided-brief-start.png" width="800" height="450" alt="Guided Brief opening screen showing the first step of the questionnaire" />

Use the Guided Brief when you need a more tailored starting point or want to share a structured brief with your client before designing.

## Step 3: Review Your Sitemap

After you click **Generate** (or complete the Guided Brief), SproutOS builds a full sitemap for your project.

<Image src="/images/getting-started/sitemap-generated.png" width="800" height="500" alt="Sitemap Editor showing auto-generated pages and sections in a visual layout" />

The sitemap shows your pages and sections in a visual layout. You can add, remove, reorder, or rename anything before moving to design. Nothing is locked in at this stage.

## Step 4: Customize Your Design

Once your sitemap is ready, SproutOS generates a full visual design for every page and section.

<Image src="/images/getting-started/design-editor.png" width="800" height="500" alt="Design Editor showing a generated website with color palette options and font pairs in the sidebar" />

In the Design Editor, you can:

- Switch between color concepts and font pairs

- Swap section layout variants

- Upload images or browse the stock photo library

- Edit text directly on the canvas

- Preview the design on mobile, tablet, and desktop

## Step 5: Export Your Project

When your design is ready, export it to your production tool.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I have to use the Guided Brief, or can I just use the quick prompt?**
Both options generate a sitemap. The quick prompt is faster. The Guided Brief gives SproutOS more context and produces a more tailored result. Use whichever fits your workflow.

**Q: Can I edit the sitemap after it is generated?**
Yes. The Sitemap Editor lets you add, remove, reorder, and rename pages and sections at any time, before and after moving to the Design Editor.

**Q: What if I don't like the design SproutOS generates?**
You can swap section variants, change color concepts, and switch font pairs inside the Design Editor. No generated design is locked in.

**Q: Do I need a paid plan to export?**
Elementor and Gutenberg exports are available on the free plan. Figma export requires the Starter plan. React export requires the Pro plan.

**Q: Can I invite my client or a team member to review the project?**
Yes. You can invite people from the dashboard and assign them a role. Clients get view-only access by default.
