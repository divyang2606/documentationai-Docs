---
title: How Do AI Credits Work in SproutOS?
description: Learn how SproutOS AI credits are used, tracked, and allocated across your workspace projects.
---

AI credits (called Sprout Tokens) are the units SproutOS uses to meter AI-powered features. Every workspace receives a token allocation based on your plan. Credits are consumed when you use AI text generation, AI design suggestions, sitemap chat, or site management actions. You can track your usage in real time from the workspace dashboard.

## What Consumes AI Credits?

| Feature | Where It Runs | Credit Usage |
|---------|--------------|--------------|
| AI text generation | Design Studio | Per generation request |
| AI design suggestions | Design Studio | Per suggestion set |
| Sitemap chat | Sitemap Planner | Per message exchange |
| Site scan and actions | Site Manager | Per action execution |
| Build module scaffolding | Site Manager | Per scaffold request |

## How to Check Your Credit Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your workspace" icon="layout">
    Go to the SproutOS Dashboard and select your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the token display" icon="bar-chart">
    Your credit balance appears in the workspace header (for example, "1.2K / 5K tokens").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the usage dashboard" icon="activity">
    Navigate to **Settings > Usage** to see a detailed breakdown by feature, AI model, and date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When Credits Run Out?

AI-powered features pause until credits reset or you upgrade your plan. You can still use non-AI features like manual text editing, section reordering, and export.

<Callout kind="tip">
  The usage dashboard color-codes your balance: blue means normal, amber means over 90% used, and red means credits are exhausted.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Expandable title="Do credits reset each month?">
  Credit allocations are tied to your billing cycle. Check your plan details in **Settings > Billing** for your specific reset date.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I buy extra credits without upgrading?">
  Contact support at support@posimyth.com to discuss add-on credit options for your workspace.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Do all team members share the same credits?">
  Yes. AI credits are allocated per workspace, not per user. All team members in a workspace draw from the same pool.
</Expandable>
