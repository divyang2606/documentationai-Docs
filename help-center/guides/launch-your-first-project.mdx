## Overview

Spin up your first automated website project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard">
    Login to your SproutOS account and click "Start New Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Project">
    Fill in name, select website type, add team members, then click "Continue".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Review settings and click "Launch Project". You'll be redirected to manage your live site.
  </Step>
</Steps>
