This guide walks you through creating a complete website project in SproutOS. You go from a blank workspace to a fully structured, AI-designed website in minutes. No design experience required.

## Prerequisites

- A SproutOS account (free trial works)
- A workspace created (SproutOS creates one automatically on first login)
- Basic information about the website you want to build (business name, goals, style preferences)

## Step 1: Create a New Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your workspace" icon="layout">
    Log in to the [SproutOS Dashboard](https://dashboard.sproutos.ai) and select your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Start New Project" icon="plus">
    Click **Start New Project** from the project dashboard. Choose how you want to begin: start from scratch, paste a website URL for analysis, upload a PDF brief, or pick a template.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you have an existing website, enter its URL. SproutOS crawls the site and auto-fills your brief with extracted colors, content, and structure.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Fill In the Guided Brief

The Guided Brief captures everything SproutOS needs to generate your website structure and design. It has six sections:

| Section | What You Provide |
|---------|-----------------|
| Project Basics | Website name, type, goals, target location |
| Business Snapshot | Value propositions, target customer, USPs, tone |
| Copy and Trust | Customer objections, available proof (testimonials, case studies) |
| Brand Direction | Style preference, logo, brand colors, typography |
| References | Websites you like (and dislike) with reasons |
| Improve Accuracy | Competitor URLs, preferred page sections |

<Callout kind="info">
  You do not need to fill every field. The more detail you provide, the more accurate the AI output. You can always come back and update the brief later.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Review and Edit the Sitemap

After completing the brief, SproutOS generates a visual sitemap showing every page and its sections.

- **Add or remove pages** by clicking the controls on the sitemap canvas
- **Reorder sections** within a page using drag-and-drop
- **Use the AI chat** on the sitemap to make changes by describing what you want (for example, "add a blog page" or "move the testimonials section above the CTA")

## Step 4: Customize in the Design Studio

The Design Studio is where your sitemap becomes a visual website. SproutOS applies AI-selected layout variants, colors, and typography based on your brief.

- **Switch layout variants** for any section to explore different designs
- **Edit text inline** by clicking on any text element
- **Replace images** by clicking on any image to browse stock photos or upload your own
- **Adjust colors and fonts** from the sidebar panels
- **Preview on desktop, tablet, and mobile** using the viewport toggle

## Step 5: Export Your Design

Once your design is ready, export it for implementation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the export panel" icon="download">
    Click **Export** in the Design Studio toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your platform" icon="layers">
    Select your export format: Elementor, Gutenberg, or Figma.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the platform instructions" icon="check">
    SproutOS provides step-by-step import instructions specific to your chosen platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Expandable title="Can I go back and edit the brief after generating a sitemap?">
  Yes. Navigate back to the Guided Brief from the project sidebar. Changes you make are reflected when you regenerate the sitemap.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How many projects can I create on the free trial?">
  The free trial includes unlimited projects. Some export formats and advanced features may require a paid plan.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I invite team members to collaborate on a project?">
  Yes. Go to your workspace settings and use the invite system to add team members with specific roles (Admin, Designer, Developer, Client, or Viewer).
</Expandable>
