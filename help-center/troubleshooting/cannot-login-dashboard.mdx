---
title: Can't Login to Dashboard
description: Resetting access in SproutOS.
---

## Symptoms

- Login form returns "Invalid credentials"
- Reset password not working

## Causes

- Incorrect email or password
- Account locked after failed logins

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify email/password">
    Make sure your credentials are correct and retry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Use "Forgot Password" to get a reset link in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If still locked out, email support@posimyth.com for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
